How to apply

The Waltair Business School offers an in-depth curriculum for both the BBA and MBA programs. Students may apply to our BBA program after Intermediate/12th class. If you are interested in one of our Graduate Programs, you must hold a Bachelor’s degree or equivalent.

online admission

The Waltair Business school recommends applying online. You benefit from an advantageous and flexible solution. Online application is quick and reliable and you will get a call-back from our staff as soon as possible.
